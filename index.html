About

Our aim is simple, let us give names to the full moons of Miami.

The names used by the rest of the country are culturally, climatically and ecologically ill suited for Miami

What does a "corn moon" mean to us? A "beaver moon"?

The full moon is associated with madness and with romance. Is there a more mad, more romantic city than Miami?

Lets plumb the depths of our incredible variety of culture, of nature, of life itself, and bequeth names to our full moons that hold meaning and capture our city's spirit
